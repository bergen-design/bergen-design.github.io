Project Overview

Category: Consulting, Design, Development

Software: Salesforce, Figma, HTML, CSS

Website: www.birks.com

I was asked to provide UI/UX consultation, ensure UI/UX best practices, and complete the site build on Salesforce with custom code for this prestigious luxury jewelry brand's international expansion.

The Challenge

Global expansion is no small feat. It comes with a special set of challenges, especially for a prestigious luxury brand looking to revamp their online presence. This is a tale of innovation, challenge, and triumph.

The prestigious luxury jewelry company faced several challenges with their international website launch:

  • UX and Accessibility Issues: The designs they were provided from another agency lacked UX best practices and accessibility criteria, necessitating consultation and redesign.
  • Platform Challenges: The website was built on a mixed platform, leading to localization issues and content cross-pollination.
  • Scope Management: Certain elements, though provided to the development agency, were not outlined in the Statement of Work (SOW), requiring additional solutions to be found.

The Solution

Thinking about design is hard, but not thinking about it can be disastrous.

I provided comprehensive UI/UX consultation and implementation, including:

  • UX Best Practices: Implemented industry-standard UX principles to enhance user experience and accessibility.
  • Custom Development: Built custom solutions on Salesforce to address the platform's limitations and requirements.
  • Localization Strategy: Developed a robust approach to handle multiple languages and regional requirements.
  • Quality Assurance: Conducted thorough testing to ensure all components met the highest standards of quality and performance.

The Results

The successful implementation of UI/UX best practices and the custom Salesforce build resulted in:

  • Improved User Experience: Delivered a seamless and intuitive interface that met the high standards expected from a luxury brand.
  • Enhanced Accessibility: Ensured the website was accessible to all users, meeting international accessibility standards.
  • Streamlined Localization: Successfully implemented a solution for managing multiple languages and regional content without cross-pollination.
  • Technical Excellence: Delivered a robust and scalable solution on the Salesforce platform with custom code that addressed all technical challenges.

Key Achievements

This project demonstrated the power of thoughtful design and technical excellence in solving complex business challenges.

  • Successfully delivered a global e-commerce platform that met the luxury brand's high standards
  • Overcame significant technical challenges related to platform limitations and localization requirements
  • Established new benchmarks for UX design and accessibility in the luxury e-commerce space
  • Created a foundation for future enhancements and expansions
  • Received positive feedback from both the client and end-users for the improved user experience

Technical Implementation

The project required careful planning and execution to ensure a smooth transition and optimal performance.

  • Phased Rollout: Implemented changes in stages to minimize disruption and allow for testing.
  • Custom Development: Created tailored solutions for unique functionality requirements.
  • Performance Optimization: Ensured fast loading times and smooth interactions across all devices.
  • Cross-Browser Compatibility: Tested and optimized for all major browsers and devices.

Wrapping Up

This project stands as a testament to the power of user-centered design in transforming digital experiences. By focusing on both aesthetics and functionality, I was able to help this luxury brand achieve remarkable business results while enhancing their digital presence.

The success of this redesign demonstrates how thoughtful UI/UX can directly impact the bottom line, even for established luxury brands with discerning customers.
